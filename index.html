Who we are


95R represent Alpha Grace Enviro-Tech (AGE) Pte Ltd (Singapore) to supply a decentralized waste processing system. The AGE proprietary design uses Alpha Magnetic Plasma technology to ionize and break down the waste by up to 95%.

What we do


All organic waste can be processed by the AMP system with minimal sorting. Inorganic such as metal, glass or concrete cannot be broken down by this system however will be collected in the bottom tray at the end of the cycle without damage to system.

Capabilities


Bio-medical waste with metal inserts (needles), plastics or tyres can all be processed in the Waste processing system. It can process up to 5 tons per day and modularity scaled up according to site requirements.
